about his Internationals

the captain of india.Rohit Sharma is an Indian cricketer known for his explosive batting and leadership skills. Born on April 30, 1987, in Bansod, Maharashtra, he has established himself as one of the premier batsmen in international cricket. Career Highlights: ODI and T20I Records: Rohit is renowned for his performances in One Day Internationals (ODIs) and Twenty20 Internationals (T20Is). He holds the record for the highest individual score in an ODI, with a staggering 264 runs against Sri Lanka in 2014. He is also known for his record three double centuries in ODIs. Captaincy: Rohit Sharma has been the captain of the Indian national cricket team in limited-overs formats and has led the team to several victories. Under his leadership, the Mumbai Indians (MI) have won multiple Indian Premier League (IPL) titles.
